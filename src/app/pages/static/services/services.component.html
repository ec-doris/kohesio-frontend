Services

In addition to the Kohesio website, we offer a number of services to access the data:

  • Query service
    Check the SPARQL endpoint to perform complex queries over Kohesio's linked open data.
  • Question answering
    Explore our experimental QA service to ask questions directly in natural language (English but also French, German, etc.)
  • Data exports
    Download all the data country by country in CSV or XLSX format.
  • EU Knowledge Graph
    Visit the Wikibase behind Kohesio and discover more about its graph structure.

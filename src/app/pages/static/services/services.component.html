Services

In addition to the Kohesio website, we offer the following services to access the data:

  • EU Knowledge Graph
    Visit the Wikibase behind Kohesio and discover more about its graph structure.
  • Data exports
    Download all the data country by country in CSV or XLSX format.

Datasets and visualisations on achievements of EU Cohesion Policy projects aggregated at programme, national, and fund level are also shared through the Cohesion Data open data platform.
